Statement of Faith

Jesus Christ

Jesus Christ is God, the living Word, who became flesh through His miraculous conception by the Holy Spirit and His virgin birth. He was crucified, buried, resurrected and ascended into Heaven and is now seated at the right hand of God the Father. Hence, he is perfect Deity and true humanity united in one person forever.

Reference verses: Matthew 1:23, Isaiah 9:6, Isaiah 44:6, Peter 1:1, John 8:24

Trinity

There is one eternal God whom we know as God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit.

Reference verses: 1 Corinthians 8:6, 1 John 4:8, Romans 1:18-20, Revelation 4:11

The Bible

The Bible is the infallible WORD OF GOD, with every answer to man's problems.

Reference verses: 2 Timothy 3:15-17, Psalms 119:9, Psalms 119:105, John 20:30-31

Salvation

The sole redemption of man is through the shed blood of Jesus Christ. We believe that on the cross of Calvary, Jesus Christ became sin, sickness, and cursed, providing both eternal salvation and divine healing for all mankind.

Reference verses: Isaiah 57:12, Hebrews 7:25, Matthew 8:18-22

Water Baptism and the Lord's Supper

We believe in the two ordinances: water baptism and the Lord's Supper, as taught and demonstrated by Jesus Christ.

Reference verses: Mark 16:16, John 3:5, Matthew 28:18-20

Sanctification

We belive in sanctification through the Word of God and work of the Holy Spirit.

Reference verses: Ephesians 2:8, James 2:18, Romans 6:19, Galatians 5:22-25

Divine Healing

We believe in divine healing; Jesus is our Healer and by His Stripes we are healed.

We believe in the authority of the Name of Jesus over demons and sickness.

Reference verses: Isaiah 53:5, Luke 9:11, Matthew 9:35, Acts 10:38, Matthew 28:18-19, Mark 16:17-28

Second Coming of Jesus

We believe in the Christian hope of the second coming of Lord Jesus Christ, the life after death and the eternal life, which begins in Heaven or Hell.

We believe that when the rapture occurs the dead in Christ will rise. Then, together with them, we who are alive and remain will meet the Lord in the air.

Reference verses: Acts 1:11, Thessalonians 4:17, Hebrews 9:28
