About Us

It was the hand of the Lord upon you that brought you to Yeshua Ministries. God looks out for us in many ways, teaching, instructing, and guiding His people in the way He chooses. We are sure He knows how greatly your life and the lives of those whom you love will be changed supernaturally through the work of the Holy Spirit that is evident through our ministries.

This verse led to the founding of Yeshua Ministries. God spoke through the verse John 3:16, which states, "For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." God's given mandate to Yeshua Ministries is to preach the love and compassion of Jesus Christ, reassure the needy, bring hope to those in despair, peace to those in need of comfort and guide the lost home to our Father.

We want you to know that we pray for you, for you truly will be blessed as you partake of the riches of God that you find here and in our meetings. We invite you to be with us in a service soon, so that you, too, can be refreshed by the presence of the Lord. Come and bring someone who needs a miracle.
